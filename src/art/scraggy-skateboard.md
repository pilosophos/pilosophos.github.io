I took a picture of the No Skateboarding sign at Ueno Park a few years ago, and I discovered it again while looking through my hard drive. Then I thought, you know, I just have a lot of these old travel photos sitting around, maybe there's something fun I can do with them. Then I remembered this [cute little MV](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZQVBPAiR5M) which mixes drawing and photography, which led me to drawing this.

Scraggy's kind of cute, don't you think? Just a little rebel to the world with no place to go.
