Let me pose this question to you: What's Mia actually wearing under that suit
with the plunging neckline? Is it a corset? A bustier? Perhaps a [bunnysuit](/art/mia-bunny-bare-legs)?
We're probably never going to find out, but here's one possibility, I guess.

This actually came about while I was studying
[Oda Non's](https://myanimelist.net/people/11266/Non_Oda) manga art (which is
usually NSFW). He draws mature women really well, and I thought I'd try emulating
it with my own pose and Mia Fey, my lawyer crush in the Ace Attorney series.
Since the sketch turned out well, I decided I'd ink and color it as well.
