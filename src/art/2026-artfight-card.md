This is the Art Fight card I'll be using for 2026. Each year they have a new theme and teams, so I chose Team Mystery (as opposed to Team Comedy and Team Tragedy) and filled out their [card template](https://artfight.net/news/116.art-fight-2026-themes-revealed).

I actually watch and read a lot more comedies than mysteries or tragedies (though I like works in all these genres), but I knew some people in the other teams already and I decided to pick one that hadn't yet been picked by them. So Mystery it is.
