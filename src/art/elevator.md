I really like evening gowns and I don't draw them enough. A lot of James Bond
movies have the girls in sexy dresses (though maybe not quite as daring as this),
and it got me thinking about a spy doing covert entry while wearing a sexy
evening gown.

Anyway, you know those keyways on elevator panels that are red and say
"Fire operation" on them? Apparently the bitting for the fire keys that go with
those keyways are often public information, so if you really wanted to, you
could 3D print your own fire key. There's YouTube videos demonstrating how the
fire service mode works too. It lets you go to any floor, bypassing credentials,
and the *door open* and *door close* buttons actually do something.

You definitely shouldn't be using your 3D printed fire key to screw around with
people's elevators, but speaking hypothetically, it is possible to use as a
covert entry method.
