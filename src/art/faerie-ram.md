This one got popular on Tumblr about a month after I originally posted it, and it has gotten occasional likes and reblogs since then. I didn't expect it to get spread around as much as it did; it was just a little something I sketched one day on a whim, without intending to finish it.

Most of the interactions it gets can be traced back to one particular reblog, which gets reblogged again by one of their followers and so on. So if you want to show an artist you like their art on Tumblr, reblogs are the way to go. (Apparently, likes don't really do anything other than give Tumblr Inc. more data on which posts engage you.)

I've not a whole lot to say about this drawing, other than I've always liked faeries and technology. Combining them is a theme I keep coming back to since I love it so damn much.
