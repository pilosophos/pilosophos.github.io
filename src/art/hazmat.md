I started playing my character [Camille](/ocs/camille), who I originally made
for Shadowrun in an RP (that isn't Shadowrun). Right now she's trespassing onto
a space station's crash site by pretending to be part of the city's hazmat
response team.

In the RP, the city's sanitation services has a contract from the fire department
to handle hazmat response. Camille takes a taxi from her hotel to the city
landfill, hacks the electronic lock on a dump truck with an employee's uniform
and ID badge inside, and disguises herself as a garbage collector.

She then quickly waves the ID card in front of a hungover guard, and walks into
a restricted zone with the hazmat equipment and truck. Driving to the crash
site, she makes it past the police checkpoint in her disguise, and starts
exploring.

The truck comes with a big drone used for scouting ahead, collecting materials
data, and decontamination.

I drew this without inking on top of an existing sketch, so the lineart is a
little sketchy in and of itself. Honestly, I kinda like doing it this way,
especially with how tedious I find doing the inking.
