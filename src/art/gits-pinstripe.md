Watched the pilot episode of the new Ghost in the Shell and it was good! Hopefully the quality keeps up for the rest of the series.

Stand Alone Complex has always been my favorite, and she's a lot more serious in that one. It'll take a bit of adjusting to the new Major.

I got to the end of the episode and I really like Motoko's pinstripe suit.
