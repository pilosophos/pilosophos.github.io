Apparently June 1st is Green(f)'s birthday and because I never keep up with these things, I only found out today, on June 2nd. But it just so happens I had to log in to the socmed to post [Hazmat](/art/hazmat) the other day, and I had some extra time on my hands to pump out a drawing. It's not too late, right? I mean, people leave their Jack-o-Lanterns out to rot for like a *month* after Halloween.

Anyway, I'm reminded of a [toot](https://tiny.tilde.website/@archenoth@chitter.xyz/116581492866224093) by my friend Archenoth that you can, at any day in the year except for one, tell someone "happy belated birthday!" and be correct. So in the grand scheme of things, my lateness is <1% of the 354 days that I could be late for this.
