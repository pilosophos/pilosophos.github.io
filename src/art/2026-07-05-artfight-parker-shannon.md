An illustration I made for Art Fight 2026, a fun little event where you draw other people's OCs, and others draw art of your OCs also.

This depicts [T0t4lly_N0t_K4thy](https://artfight.net/~T0t4lly_N0t_K4thy)'s Parker Shannon, a self-professed delinquent.

I'm using Art Fight as an opportunity to try some new things-- experiment with brushes, techniques, that sort of thing. I did a really rough sketch, put some paint down, and put some rudimentary lineart over the paint. As I was switching back and forth doing this to finish different parts of the illustration, I accidentally got the lineart and paint layers mixed up. I had done enough work on the wrong layer by accident that I just decided to embrace the chaos and work on all of it as one layer, which in retrospect mainly made things less convenient.

Well, whatever. Live and learn.
