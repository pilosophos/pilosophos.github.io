An illustration I made for Art Fight 2026, a fun little event where you draw other people's OCs, and others draw art of your OCs also.

This depicts [Firebet](https://artfight.net/~firebet)'s Sibot, a man-at-arms who was changed into a monster after wearing the pelt of a legendary creature for too long.

The horse is a study of a horse drawn by Endou Okito, who is a longtime inspiration of mine. I don't draw animals very often, so I think I'm going to need more practice.
